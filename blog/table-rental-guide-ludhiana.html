Complete Guide to Table Rental in Ludhiana

Tables are the foundation of any successful event setup. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or birthday party, choosing the right tables is essential for guest comfort and event flow. As a leading table rental provider in Ludhiana, Satya Tent Agency offers comprehensive table solutions for all types of events.

Types of Tables Available

1. Round Tables

Best for: Weddings, receptions, social gatherings

  • Seats 8-10 guests comfortably
  • Promotes conversation and interaction
  • Elegant appearance for formal events
  • Available in various sizes (4ft, 5ft, 6ft)
  • Perfect for family-style dining

Price range: ₹100-200 per table per day

2. Rectangle Tables

Best for: Corporate events, conferences, buffet setups

  • Space-efficient for large gatherings
  • Easy to arrange in rows
  • Perfect for buffet and display setups
  • Available in 6ft and 8ft lengths
  • Cost-effective for large events

Price range: ₹80-150 per table per day

3. Square Tables

Best for: Intimate gatherings, cocktail parties

  • Seats 4 guests comfortably
  • Modern and stylish appearance
  • Great for cocktail setups
  • Easy to arrange in various patterns
  • Perfect for small group conversations

Price range: ₹60-120 per table per day

4. High Tables (Bar Tables)

Best for: Cocktail parties, standing receptions

  • Perfect for standing and mingling
  • Great for cocktail and appetizer service
  • Modern and trendy appearance
  • Space-efficient for large crowds
  • Ideal for networking events

Price range: ₹120-200 per table per day

Table Selection Guide

Event Type Considerations

  • Weddings: Mix of round tables for dining, rectangle tables for buffet
  • Corporate Events: Rectangle tables for presentations, round tables for networking
  • Birthday Parties: Round tables for family dining, high tables for bar area
  • Religious Functions: Rectangle tables for community dining
  • Cocktail Parties: High tables and small round tables

Venue Space Planning

  • Measure your venue: Know exact dimensions and capacity
  • Consider traffic flow: Leave adequate space between tables
  • Plan for service areas: Buffet tables, bar areas, dessert stations
  • Accessibility: Ensure wheelchair access and clear pathways

Table Quantity Planning

Calculate the right number of tables for your event:

Table Covers and Linens

Enhance your tables with beautiful covers and linens:

Table Cover Types

  • Polyester Covers: Affordable, easy to clean, various colors
  • Linen Covers: Premium look, natural texture, elegant drape
  • Silk Covers: Luxurious appearance, perfect for weddings
  • Plastic Covers: Waterproof, perfect for outdoor events

Table Linen Options

  • Table Runners: Decorative centerpieces for formal events
  • Table Skirts: Hide table legs for elegant appearance
  • Overlays: Additional decorative layers
  • Napkins: Coordinating napkins for complete look

Table Setup and Arrangement

Wedding Table Arrangements

  • Head Table: Special table for bride, groom, and wedding party
  • Family Tables: Reserved tables for immediate family
  • Guest Tables: Round tables for general guests
  • Buffet Tables: Rectangle tables for food service

Corporate Event Setup

  • Presentation Setup: Rectangle tables in rows facing stage
  • Boardroom Setup: Large rectangle table for meetings
  • Networking Setup: Mix of round and high tables
  • Exhibition Setup: Rectangle tables for displays

Cost-Saving Tips

Maximize value while staying within budget:

  • Mix Table Types: Use premium tables for VIP areas, standard tables for general seating
  • Rent in Bulk: Negotiate better rates for large quantities
  • Seasonal Booking: Book during off-season for better rates
  • Package Deals: Combine table rental with chairs and other services
  • Local Suppliers: Choose local suppliers to save on transportation

Quality and Maintenance

Ensure you get quality tables:

  • Inspection: Check tables for stability and cleanliness
  • Level Surfaces: Ensure all tables are level and stable
  • Clean Condition: All tables should be clean and presentable
  • Consistent Style: All tables should match in style and condition
  • Backup Tables: Have extra tables available for last-minute needs
