üéØ Tips for Perfect Wedding Decoration

Plan in Advance

Start planning your decoration at least 3-4 months before the wedding to ensure availability of materials and vendors.

Choose a Color Scheme

Select 2-3 main colors that complement your wedding theme and use them consistently throughout the decoration.

Consider Photography

Ensure your decoration looks great in photos by choosing colors and lighting that enhance photography.

Guest Comfort

Prioritize guest comfort with proper seating, ventilation, and easy access to facilities.
