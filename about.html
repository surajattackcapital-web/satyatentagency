Our Story

Leading Tent House in Ludhiana & Punjab

Founded in 2008, Satya Tent Agency has been serving Punjab with excellence in event management, tent rental, catering, and decoration services. What started as a small tent rental business in Ludhiana has now grown into one of Punjab's most trusted names in the event industry.

Over the past 15+ years, we have successfully organized more than 5000 events including weddings, corporate functions, religious ceremonies, and social gatherings across Punjab. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and attention to detail has made us the preferred choice for event services in Ludhiana, Jalandhar, Amritsar, and beyond.

Today, Satya Tent Agency stands as a comprehensive event solutions provider with a team of experienced professionals, premium quality materials, and a track record of delivering memorable events.
